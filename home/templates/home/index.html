Welcome to Community Fitness

Gym Fit is a community based fitness centre designed to bring people together to enjoy the experience of group fitness, reap the benefits of having a healthier lifestyle and meet like minded people along the way.

To get started, view Our Classes, to see what classes we offer, and maybe add a few to your favourites list, and then go to our schedule of Classes This Week, purchase a class and come down and get involved. If you like what we have to offer, and want to become a regular, then Join Us by purchasing one of our Class Access Packages.

Our classes generally run from:

8am to 9pm Monday to Friday

8am to 6pm on Weekends

To come and take part in our classes you can either purchase classes individually, through this website, on a pay as you go system or have a look at our Class Access Packages. These either contain a set number of Class Tokens, which you can use to book on to any class, or they give you Unlimited Use of our classes.

If you have any questions Get In Touch or come down and chat to us
